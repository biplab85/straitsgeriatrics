## 9. Recommended Team Composition

### Option A: Solo Full-Stack Developer
One senior developer with strong frontend, SEO, and accessibility skills. Best for smaller budget.

**Must-have skills:** Next.js/Astro, Tailwind CSS, SEO, Accessibility, Responsive Design

### Option B: Small Team (Recommended)
| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| **Frontend Developer** | Build all pages, components, responsive design, performance |
| **UI/UX Designer** | Design system, wireframes, visual design, accessibility review |
| **SEO Specialist** | Technical SEO, structured data, content optimization, local SEO |
| **Content Writer** | Blog articles, medical content review, copywriting |

### Option C: Agency Team
Full-service delivery including design, development, SEO, content, and ongoing maintenance.
